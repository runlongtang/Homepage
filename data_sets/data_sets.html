Data Sets

There is no Statistics without data. Probability is a mathematical language to describe randomness. It is the language used in statistical theories. The statistical theories reveal properties of statistical methods. The statistical methods are invented and developed to analyze real data with the ultimate goal of helping us solve various real-world problems. Data is like soil to plants and thus fundamentally important in Statistics.

The following is a list of exploratory data analysis (EDA) of some data sets. EDA is simple but a necessary step for all data analysis. Before applying or developing statistical methods or algorithms, we need to have a basic idea of what the data is and what the variables look like.

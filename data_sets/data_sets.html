Data Sets

There is no Statistics without data. All the statistical theories are for statistical methods. All the statistical methods are invented and developed to analyze data with the fundamental goal of helping solve various real-world problems. So, data is fundamentally important in Statistics.

The following is a list of exploratory data analysis (EDA) of some data sets. EDA is simple but a necessary step for all data analysis. Before applying statistical methods or algorithms, we need to have a basic idea of what the data is and what the variables look like.

  • 2019-01-30: bcw (Breast Cancer Wisconsin)
